  \textbf{Linear Equation}\\
  $ax_1+a_2x_3+....+a_nx_n=b$, where x represents variables and b is given.

  \textbf{System of Linear Equation}\\
  $a_{11}x_1+a_{12}x_3+....+a_{1n}x_n=b$\\
  $a_{21}x_{21}+a_{22}x_3+....+a_{2n}x_n=b_2$\\
  $ax_{31}+a_{32}x_3+....+a_{3n}x_n=b_3$\\
  $a_{m1}x_1+a_{m2}x_2+...+a_{mn}x_{n}=b_m$

  \textbf{Elementary Raw Operations}\\
  1. Replacement - replace one row by the sum of itself and a multiple of another row.\\
  2. Interchange - Interchange two rows\\
  3. Multiply all entries in a row by a non zero constant

  \textbf{Ex 1}
  \[
    3x+4y=5
  \]
  \[
    2x-7y=3
  \]
  \[
    \begin{bmatrix}
      3 &4 & 5\\
      2 &-7 & 3
    \end{bmatrix} \to
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 &11 & 2\\
      2 &-7 & 3
    \end{bmatrix}
   \to 
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 & 11 & 2\\
      0 &-29 &-1
    \end{bmatrix}
  \]
  \textbf{Ex 2 Gaussian Elimination}
  \[
    \begin{aligned}
      x_1-2x_2+x_3=0\\
      2x_2-8x_3=8\\
      5x_1-5x_3=10\\
    \end{aligned}
  \]
  \[
    \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & -2 & 1 & 0\\
    0 & 2 & -8 & 8\\
    5 &0 & 5 & 10
    \end{bmatrix}
    \to
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 &-2 & 1 & 0\\
      0 &2 & -8 & 8\\
      0 &10 & -10 & 10
    \end{bmatrix}
    \to 
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 & -2 &1 &0\\
      0 &1 & -4 &4\\
      0 &10 & -10 & 10
    \end{bmatrix}
  \]
  \[
    \to \text{scale by } \frac{1}{3}
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 &0 &-7 & 8\\
      0 &1 & -4 &4\\
      0 &0 &30 &-30
    \end{bmatrix} \to
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1 &0 &-7 & 8\\
      0 &1 &-4 &4\\
      0 &0 &1 &-1
    \end{bmatrix}
  \]
